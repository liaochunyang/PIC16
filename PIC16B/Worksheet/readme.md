# Worksheets

In some quarters, I included worksheets for discussion sections. These worksheets are intended to supplement the lecture notes and provide additional practice and examples.

In the original course offerings, grading was based primarily on completeness rather than correctness. As a result, the worksheets may contain typos, mistakes, ambiguities, incomplete solutions, or other inaccuracies.

The materials are provided as-is and should not be considered authoritative references. Users are encouraged to verify results independently and use them at their own risk.
